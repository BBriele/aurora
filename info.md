# 🌅 Aurora

A smart, modular alarm clock for Home Assistant — **one product for thousands of different setups**.

Aurora works on *any* setup: from just a phone or a single `media_player`, up to a kiosk tablet + smartwatch + AI vision + smart lights. Rich hardware **enables** features; it is never required. Anything you don't have degrades gracefully — and **the exact alarm time is never missed**.

- **Capability-first:** the core knows abstract *roles* (speaker, sunrise light, notification, sleep signal, vision provider…), never specific brands. Bind your own devices; leave the rest empty.
- **Setup via UI, no YAML.** Add/remove alarms like on your phone.
- **Bundled Lovelace card**, auto-registered by the integration.

> ⚠️ In active development. See the README for the phase roadmap.

After installation, restart Home Assistant and add **Aurora** from *Settings → Devices & Services*.
